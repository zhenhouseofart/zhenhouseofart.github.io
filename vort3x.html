CLIENT

VORT3X

VORT3X is a Malaysian creative agency founded in 2013. While they focus on brand identity design, their goals are to create successful brands, campaigns and marketing strategies for their clients. They have worked with several known brands such as Gamuda, EcoWorld, Sunway, Setia and Fave.

Every year, VORT3X creates stunning red packet designs for the Chinese New Years that are sought after by collectors and enthusiasts. Their designs have also been discovered to be bid by resellers on local platforms such as Carousell.

I was tasked with their red packet design for the year of 2021, which was the year of the cow and also the second year into Covid. The theme for the year was the Beijing Opera (visually) and the three Sanxing deities (conceptually). After research into the common bill size of Malaysia, Singapore and Indonesia, I drafted and tested several renditions, where the final print was on sticker paper, finished with silver foil and assembled without sealants but with simple clasps. Cleverly, the name of the Angpao set was titled “Topeng Mooka”, which in Malay, topeng muka means face mask. The mu had been substituted for moo for the year of the cow.
