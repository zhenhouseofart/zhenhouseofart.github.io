Brand Identity Design for Malaysian Local Food Vendor

MY ROLES

Lead Designer

Project Proposal

CLIENT

WeLove Food Enterprise

WeLove Food Enterprise is a local food vendor founded in Ipoh Canning Garden and have been in business since 1985. They specialise in Chee Chong Fun and other Malaysian delights. The franchise had been passed down to the founder’s son, who had sought a rebrand and a marketing strategy in an attempt to further grow the business, although they did not want a visual that strayed too far from the olden Malaysian Chinese look.

The project deliverables were a logo suite, identity guidelines, a marketing strategy and collaterals such as aprons, caps and t-shirts for staff use.
