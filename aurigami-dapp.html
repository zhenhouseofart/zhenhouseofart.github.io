CLIENT

Aurigami Finance

Aurigami is a Decentralised Finance Application (DApp) on the Aurora blockchain. It is a liquidity protocol (LP) that functions as an Automated Money Market (AMM). Users will deposit their assets into the protocol and be rewarded with variable yield (APY) which can be understood as interest. Depositors can then choose to lend and/or borrow assets in an over-collateralised fashion.

Prior to this project, Aurigami’s DApp interface was still in a relatively primitive state. After studying Aurigami by using it and several other protocols, I noted down any struggles or ease I had whilst using them. Problems unique to Aurigami could be solved by observing and implementing solutions adopted by other similar protocols. However, more prominent issues such as mobile responsiveness, seemed to be an ecosystem wide struggle, where some sort of compromise always had to be made.

I was entrusted with the redevelopment of the Aurigami DApp’s UI and UX. I managed my team by working closely with the product owner to gauge his vision and with the front end developer to achieve an excellent, responsive webapp within the allocated time frame. Much of this success must also be accredited to the advisory given by experienced long term users.

Not long after the launch, the development team and I were reached out by one of the veterans in the field for an interview regarding Aurigami’s new look and user interface. The DApp was reported to be intuitive and easy on the eyes.
